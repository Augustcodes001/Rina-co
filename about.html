Background Story

Rina and Co consults Limited founded in the year 2023, is a fully registered firm that operates in compliance with all applicable Nigerian laws and regulations. The company hold valid registration with cooperate affairs commission and partners with professionals in legal, finance, construction and estate management.

We render services in real estate, investment, and building construction. As a firm, we are committed to excellence in the acquisition, development, and sales of real estate across Nigeria. With a track record of successful land sales and development projects. The firm offers structured real estate investment opportunities with guaranteed returns.

Rina and Co. consults Limited is committed to transparency, investors safety, modern building development, and structured real estate growth.

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that shape our approach to real estate development

Our Mission

To transform the real estate landscape by developing sustainable communities that offer exceptional value, comfort, and security. We are committed to excellence in every project, leveraging innovative design and quality construction to exceed client expectations.

Our Vision

To be Nigeria's most trusted real estate developer, recognized for creating thriving communities that enhance quality of life. We envision a future where every Nigerian has access to well-designed, affordable properties in secure, well-planned neighborhoods.

Integrity
