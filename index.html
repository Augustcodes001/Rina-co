Our Story & Vision

Founded in 2010, Rina & CO. Consults has grown from a small boutique real estate agency to a leading developer of premium residential communities. Our journey began with a single vision: to create spaces that inspire, connect, and elevate quality of life.

Today, we've successfully developed over 25 communities across the region, each embodying our core values of innovation, sustainability, and community building. We believe in creating more than just buildings - we craft environments where families thrive and memories are made.

Our team of architects, engineers, and community planners work collaboratively to design spaces that harmonize with their surroundings while providing modern comforts and sustainable features.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our properties and services.

What makes Rina & CO. developments different from others?

Our developments focus on creating complete communities rather than just building houses. We prioritize sustainable materials, smart home technology, community spaces, and long-term value. Each project undergoes meticulous planning to ensure it meets our high standards for design, functionality, and environmental responsibility.

What financing options do you offer?

We offer flexible financing solutions to suit different needs, including traditional mortgages through our partner banks, in-house financing with competitive rates, and customized payment plans. Our financial advisors can help you explore options such as fixed-rate mortgages, adjustable-rate mortgages, and special first-time homebuyer programs.

Can I customize my home design?

Absolutely! Our customization studio allows you to personalize your home to match your lifestyle and preferences. You can choose from various floor plans, select finishes and materials, upgrade appliances, and even modify room layouts during the pre-construction phase. Our design consultants will guide you through the process to ensure your vision becomes reality.

What is your construction timeline for new developments?

Construction timelines vary based on the project scale and complexity. Typically, single-family homes take 8-12 months from groundbreaking to completion, while larger multi-unit developments may take 18-24 months. We provide regular progress updates and maintain strict quality control throughout the construction process to ensure timely delivery without compromising on quality.

Do you offer warranty on your properties?

Yes, all our properties come with a comprehensive 10-year structural warranty and a 2-year warranty on fixtures, fittings, and appliances. Additionally, we provide a 30-day post-move-in service to address any initial adjustments you might need. Our commitment to quality extends beyond the sale, with a dedicated customer care team available throughout your ownership.
