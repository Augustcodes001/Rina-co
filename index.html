What makes Rina & CO. developments different from others?

Our developments focus on creating complete communities rather than just building houses. We prioritize sustainable materials, community spaces, and long-term value. Each project undergoes meticulous planning to ensure it meets our high standards for design, functionality, and environmental responsibility.

What financing options do you offer?

We offer flexible financing solutions to suit different needs, including a 3 tier installmental payments for lands on our estate and customized payment plans. Our financial advisors can help you explore options that suits your budget.

What is your construction timeline for new developments?

Construction timelines vary based on the project scale and complexity.
